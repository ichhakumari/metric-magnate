FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our design and branding process.

01

How long does a brand identity project take?

A typical brand identity project takes 3-6 weeks, depending on complexity. This includes research, concept development, and finalization.

02

What's a UI/UX design process?

We follow a structured process: user research, wireframing, high-fidelity design, prototyping, usability testing, and developer handoff.

03

What files do I receive?

You get all source files (AI, PSD, Figma), exports in all formats (SVG, PNG, PDF), and a comprehensive brand guidelines document.

04

Can you redesign an existing brand?

Absolutely. We specialize in brand refreshes and full rebrands that modernize your identity while preserving brand equity and recognition.

05

Do you handle web development too?

Yes, we offer end-to-end design and development. Our designers and developers work together to bring your vision to life pixel-perfectly.

06

How many design revisions do you offer?

We include 3 rounds of revisions for each deliverable. Our process ensures alignment from the start, so revisions are minimal and focused.
